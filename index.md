# Privacy Policy for Waves

Effective date: July 11, 2026

Waves is a focus timer developed by Keith Kessens.

## Information We Collect

Waves does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

The app does not use advertising, analytics, tracking technologies, or third-party analytics SDKs.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Waves stores focus history, focus scores, timer state, app preferences, and onboarding status locally on your device.

This information is not transmitted to the developer or shared with third parties. Deleting Waves removes this locally stored information.

## Purchases

Waves offers optional subscriptions through Apple’s StoreKit system. Apple processes purchases and may process purchase-related information according to [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/
privacy/).

Waves accesses only the StoreKit transaction information necessary to determine whether Premium access is active.

## Tracking

Waves does not track users across apps or websites.

## Children’s Privacy

Waves does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if Waves’ features or data practices change. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact [wavesappsupport@gmail.com](mailto:wavesappsupport@gmail.com).
